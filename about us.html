ABOUT US

Our Story

In the bustling heart of Colombo, Shevon, Senuth and Deegayu noticed a growing challenge: the struggle of finding quality products tailored to individual needs. This inspired her to create a revolutionary platform that would transform the shopping experience.

In 2009, from a small office in Colombo, BuyAnything.com was born. The team worked tirelessly, leveraging AI and machine learning to analyze customer behavior and offer personalized recommendations.

The platform quickly gained traction, attracting a loyal customer base. From electronics to fashion, automotive to groceries, BuyAnything.com became the go-to destination for Sri Lankan consumers.

Supported by a wide range of tailored marketing, data, and service solutiond, BuyAnything.com has 40,000 sellers, 600 brands, more than 3 million products and services 5.5 million consumers across the region.

Today, BuyAnything.com stands as a testament to vision and dedication.

Our Mission

At BuyAnything.com, our mission is to provide an unparalleled online shopping experience by offering a vast selection of high-quality products, exceptional customer service, and competitive prices.

We are committed to making shopping convenient, enjoyable, and accessible to everyone, while fostering a community of trust and reliability. Our goal is to continually innovate and enhance our platform to meet the ever-evolving needs of our customers, ensuring they can find and buy anything they desire with ease and confidence.
